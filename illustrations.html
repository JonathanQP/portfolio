A showcase of various illustrations.

On this page I want to show a couple of different illustration styles I've worked in. Most of these illustrations were created in Adobe Illustrator or Adobe Photoshop.

I want to draw special attention to the edit of the Mona Lisa, because while it did use existing imagery at its base it's not a simple photo edit. These photographs were converted to scalable SVG illustrations in Illustrator to create an infinitely scaleable kind of pop art.

I believe this page conveys I possess a good amount of flexibility in my design styles.
