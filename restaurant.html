For this project I was tasked to create a theoretical rebranding for a restaurant of my choice.

You can see multiple realistic mockups. I made sure to create a wide variety of applications of the new branding guidelines, in order to sell the concept of the rebranding as well as possible.

I chose to create designs for Kioku, a restaurant in Antwerp that offers a variety of Asian cuisine.

Since Kioku offers a variety of Asian cuisine with a focus on Japanese dishes and seafood, I decided wanted to showcase a degree of fluidity. Water felt like a thematic match, so I chose a calligraphic watercolour style that brings lively waves to mind.

You can see Kioku's actual branding on their website.
It's serviceable, but I believe my work would make for a considerable upgrade.
